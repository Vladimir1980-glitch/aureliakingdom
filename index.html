About the Kingdom

Aurelian Kingdom, the land of harmony and light, was founded by King Evandor I over a thousand years ago. It is divided into five duchies, each with unique landscapes, resources, and culture.

Explore the Duchies

  • Duchy of Lumiria - Known for its golden mines and ancient temples.
  • Duchy of Viridia - Lush forests and rare flora and fauna.
  • Duchy of Infernia - Volcanic lands rich in minerals.
  • Duchy of Aquelia - Home to rivers, lakes, and vibrant trade routes.
  • Duchy of Aurelinum - The cultural and magical heart of the kingdom.
